Reserved Private IP Ranges
==========================

Three ranges of IP addresses that have been declared as
private. Networks may use them internally as they wish. The only rule
is that no packets containing these addresses may appear on the
Internet itself.

  10.0.0.0     -   10.255.255.255/8   (16,777,216 hosts)
  172.16.0.0   -   172.31.255.255/12  ( 1,048,576 hosts)
  192.168.0.0  -   192.168.255.255/16 (     65536 hosts)
